1121 BASMATI RICE

The 1121 Basmati Rice is the world’s longest grain rice in Basmati Rice family. The length of uncooked 1121 basmati ranges from 8.05 to 8.30mm. The varieties like 1121 Basmati White rice, 1121 basmati brown rice, 1121 sella(Parboiled) Rice and 1121 Basmati Steam Rice are extremely popular in the market.

HMT RICE

HMT Rice, also known as HMT Kolam Rice, is a popular and widely available variety in India. Grown primarily in Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh, HMT Rice is known for its long and slender grains, delicate fragrance, and superior cooking properties. It is highly sought after for its exceptional quality, taste, and ability to absorb flavors, making it perfect for biryanis, pulaos, and other rice-based dishes. With its widespread availability in markets and grocery stores across the country, HMT Rice continues to be a preferred choice for those seeking a premium rice variety that adds elegance to their meals.
